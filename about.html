About Bored Tasks

We're on a slightly ridiculous, completely sincere mission: to make sure no one is ever bored again.

Bored Tasks started with a feeling everyone knows too well - that restless, scrolling-with-nothing-to-watch, "I'm so bored but I don't know what to do" feeling. We'd open our phones, flick through the same five apps, and somehow feel more bored than when we started. We figured there had to be a better cure for the slow afternoons, the long commutes, the "we're waiting for the food to arrive" lulls. So we built one.

Today, Bored Tasks is a playground for curious, restless, fun-loving people. We make wildly shareable personality quizzes, brain-tickling trivia, and a constantly-growing library of hundreds of things to do when boredom strikes - sorted by mood, energy, and how much effort you can realistically muster. Whether you want to discover your love language, find out which decade you secretly belong in, or just get a random task that gets you off the couch, we've got you.

What we actually believe

Boredom isn't the enemy - wasted boredom is. A bored brain is a brain looking for a spark, and we think that spark should be fun, harmless, and occasionally hilarious. Our quizzes are designed to be that perfect mix of "this is so accurate it's scary" and "I'm screenshotting this for the group chat." Our activity ideas are designed to be genuinely doable, not Pinterest fantasies that require a craft store and three free hours.

We also believe in quality. Every quiz is written by real people who care about whether the questions are fun and the results feel true. Every activity is something we'd actually do ourselves. We're not interested in being a thin little quiz farm - we want Bored Tasks to be a place you bookmark, come back to, and send to your friends at 11pm with the caption "this is literally you."

Our values

  • Fun first. If it isn't genuinely enjoyable, it doesn't make the cut.
  • Made for sharing. The best moments happen when you compare results with friends.
  • Inclusive & kind. Our quizzes are for entertainment, never to judge or label anyone.
  • Mobile-perfect. Most of you find us from a link in a story or a DM, so we obsess over the phone experience.
  • Respectful of your time and data. Your quiz progress is saved on your device, not ours.

A quick, important note

Bored Tasks is built purely for entertainment. Our personality quizzes are playful, not psychological assessments - discovering you're "emotionally a moody teenager" is meant to make you laugh, not to diagnose anything. If you're looking for real support around mental health, relationships, or wellbeing, please talk to a qualified professional. We're here for the fun part.
